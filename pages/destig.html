Instructions:

Please read these instructions carefully and do not communicate with anyone while you are making your decisions.

  • You will earn $10 by participating in this research that you may keep and/or use to purchase food or drink products. You may think of this money as a bank account from which you can withdraw money.
  • Depending on the decisions you make, you may receive a combination of cash and food or drink products.
  • Your decisions are just like the ones you make in a store: you either buy the food or drink product at the listed price or you do not.
  • There are no greater physical risks from participating in this study than those you would face in a store. Please remember that all decisions are real purchasing decisions, therefore, one of your purchasing decisions will be randomly selected and implemented

Steps:

  1. You will face a series of "options" to purchase food. For each option, decide if you want to buy the food or drink product at the listed price by selecting "Yes" or "No".
  2. Complete a short survey.
  3. Roll a digital die to determine which purchasing option will be implemented (only one will be implemented).
  4. Receive cash and/or food or drink product.

  • Example 1: If you selected 'Yes' for an option that cost $3 and this option is implemented, you will receive the food or drink product and $7 cash ($10 - $3 = $7).
  • Example 2: If you selected 'No' for an option and this option is implemented, you will receive $10 cash and will not receive any food or drink product.
